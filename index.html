The problem: your home is left unwatched

When the family is at work, on holiday or simply away, the home is vulnerable and often completely "invisible" to the owners. Most break-ins happen through windows and the front door – exactly when there's no one to react in time and no real information about what's going on inside.

  • No one sees the break-in attempt in real time.
  • Reaction comes too late – after the damage is done.
  • Standard alarms often have no link to the owner and just sound into empty space.
  • You don't know if windows and doors are closed when you rush to work or travel.
Aura HomeSystems

Our solution: smart sensors with real alerts

The sensors are super easy to install and configure – you mount them on windows and entrance doors, they connect to the app and instantly monitor status in real time. On any change you get instant notifications on your phone. Our app is extremely user-friendly: a clear signal of which opening was affected, when it happened, and how long it stayed open.

  • You see in real time whether each point is open or closed.
  • You get clear notifications for any suspicious activity.
  • You can react immediately – wherever you are.

What you get

Aura HomeSystems is sensors and app only – easy setup from the window to the phone notification.

1) Sign up or log in to the Aura app on your phone. 2) Mount the sensor on the door or window: one part on the frame, the other on the opening part, so the magnets face each other when closed. 3) In the app tap "Add sensor" and follow the steps: select your home Wi‑Fi (2.4 GHz) and enter the password. 4) Give the sensor a name (e.g. "Front door", "Kids' window") and save. 5) Enable notifications for Aura in your phone settings (Settings → Apps → Aura → Notifications). After that, you'll get an instant notification on your phone whenever something opens or closes.
For reliable alerts, allow notifications for the Aura app, set battery usage to unrestricted or disable battery optimization for Aura, allow background activity and keep mobile data or WiFi enabled.
